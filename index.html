Why Technology? Technology is the backbone of modern life.

By equipping myself with these skills, I’m preparing to be part of the future, creating tools that can simplify processes, solve real problems, and make the world more connected.

My Goals: My short-term goal is to master the foundations of web development and create projects that showcase my skills.
In the long term, I want to become a well-rounded Full Stack Engineer who contributes to open-source projects, mentors upcoming developers, and eventually builds scalable applications that can impact businesses and communities.

Why This Journey Matters: For me, this journey is not just about writing code.
It’s about proving to myself that with dedication and consistency, I can achieve expertise in one of the most in-demand skills in the world.
It’s about growth, resilience, and the drive to keep learning. Every line of code I write today takes me one step closer to my dream of becoming a professional software engineer.
